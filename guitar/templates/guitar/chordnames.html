Chord Notation


Chord notation is quite conventional e.g. C Gm F#7 Db.

- The root note (A..G) must be uppercase - Seperate every item with spaces - # indicates sharp - b (lowercase B) indicates flat - m (lowercase) indicates minor - maj (lowercase) indicates major - sus (lowercase) indicates suspended - dim (lowercase) indicates diminished - aug (lowercase) indicates augmented - add (lowercase) indicates extensions Variations on the root chord: m, 6, m6, 69, 7, m7, maj7, 7b5, 7#5, m7b5, 7b9, 9, m9, maj9, add9, 13, sus2, sus4, dim, dim7, aug
